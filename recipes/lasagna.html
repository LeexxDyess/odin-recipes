Lasagna

Description

An easy recipe for a tasty, yet easy, homemade lasagna. Made with
tomato sauce, meat, and various cheeses...it'll be a recipe your family
will be beggging you to make for years to come!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 Jar Spaghetti Sauce
  • 32 Oz Cottage Cheese
  • 3 Cups Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesan
  • 2 Tsp dried Parsley
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Pinch Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles Al Dente
  • 1/2 Cup Water
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees F

    2. Brwon Ground Beef in skillet over Medium High.
      Drain and Discard Grease. Stir in spaghetti sauce.

    3. Combine Cottage Cheese, 2 Cups of Mozarella, eggs,
      half the grated parmesan, dried parsley, salt, and pepper
      in a large bowl.

    4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover
      with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cups of cheese mixture,
      and 1/4 cup sauce; repeat layers once more. Top with remaining
      3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese. Pour 1/2 cup
      water along the edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.

    5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an
      additional 10 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

    6. Serve and enjoy!
